comparison - tinyux vs FullStory

A minimal FullStory alternative when replay is too much

tinyux is a self-hosted option for teams that need contextual UX events and surveys without adopting a full digital experience analytics platform.

Try the demo Read the docs
ALT - 01

What FullStory does

FullStory

FullStory is a digital experience analytics platform with session replay, behavioral search, journey analysis, and enterprise-oriented workflows for finding friction across products.

tinyux

tinyux is intentionally narrower. It does not reconstruct sessions or provide a search UI. It records the events you choose, optional aggregate heatmap points, and the answers users submit, then leaves analysis to your database or internal tools.

ALT - 03

Which should you choose?

Choose FullStory if...

you need high-fidelity session replay, deep behavioral analysis, team workflows, and managed analytics infrastructure.

Choose tinyux if...

you want a readable script that captures just enough UX context for a small product or privacy-sensitive workflow.
