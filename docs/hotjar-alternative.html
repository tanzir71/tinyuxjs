comparison - tinyux vs Hotjar

A tiny self-hosted Hotjar alternative for targeted feedback

tinyux is a small self-hosted alternative when you need targeted UX events and contextual surveys, not heatmaps or session replay.

Try the demo Read the docs
ALT - 01

What Hotjar does

Hotjar

Hotjar, now part of Contentsquare, is built around behavior analytics such as heatmaps, session replay, funnels, surveys, and feedback workflows. It is useful when teams want visual behavior analysis and a managed platform.

tinyux

tinyux does not do heatmaps or replay. It records selected interaction signals and micro-survey answers, then posts them to your own collector. The trade is simple: less product, less data surface, more ownership.

ALT - 03

Which should you choose?

Choose Hotjar if...

you need heatmaps, replay, funnel views, feedback widgets, and a managed workspace for non-technical teammates.

Choose tinyux if...

you only need to instrument important moments, ask contextual questions, and keep raw data on your server.
