comparison - tinyux vs PostHog

A tiny PostHog alternative when you do not need a product OS

tinyux is for teams that need one script, one collector, and contextual feedback. PostHog is a much broader product engineering platform.

Try the demo Read the docs
ALT - 01

What PostHog does

PostHog

PostHog combines product analytics, web analytics, session replay, feature flags, experiments, surveys, data tools, and other product engineering workflows.

tinyux

tinyux is not trying to be a product operating system. It captures lightweight events and survey answers, then stores them where you decide.

ALT - 03

Which should you choose?

Choose PostHog if...

you want product analytics, flags, experiments, replay, surveys, and warehouse-style tooling in one platform.

Choose tinyux if...

you need a small self-hosted telemetry layer and would rather not define a full analytics taxonomy yet.
