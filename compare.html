CMP - 03

Choose the smaller thing only when it is enough

tinyux is intentionally incomplete. That is the point.

Choose tinyux if...

You need a quick self-hosted answer to "what did people click, where did they stall, and what did they say right then?" You can inspect JSON rows or build your own dashboard later.

Choose a platform if...

You need replay search, heatmaps, funnels, account analytics, feature flagging, long-term product metrics, team permissions, or vendor-managed reporting.
